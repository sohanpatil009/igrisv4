LORE

Igris was originally a knight of the Demon Castle — the captain of the Demon Army's elite guard. He served under the Demon King Baran, wielding a massive sword and armor that struck fear into all who faced him.

During the Jeju Island Red Gate incident, Igris confronted Sung Jin-Woo in a ferocious battle within the castle's throne room. Despite his overwhelming strength and centuries of combat experience, he was ultimately defeated by Jin-Woo's growing power and tactical genius.

After his defeat, Igris was resurrected as a shadow soldier through Jin-Woo's Arise ability. Unlike lesser shadows, Igris retained his full intellect, personality, and combat mastery — a mark of his exceptional will even in death.

He became the first commander-rank shadow in Jin-Woo's Shadow Army, leading countless battles against the Ant King, the Monarchs, and the forces of the Rulers. His loyalty to the Shadow Monarch is absolute, and his blade remains ever ready.
